Scrum is an agile framework, there used to organize the product better

Process:
1. a product owner creates the wishlist
2. during sprint plannning, the team pulls a chunk from the list and decides how to complete it
3. The team has a set time frame  (the sprint),  to complete the work, they meet in a daily scrum to keep
the work moving forward, the scrum master keeps the process moving forward.
4. At the end of the sprint the work should be shippable
5. The team conducts a sprint review on the product and a retrospective on the process
6. The scrum does another backlog (wishlist) if the process isn't complete
