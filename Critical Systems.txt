safety, reliability, availability, security

safety is when a system should never harm people
reliability is the ability of the system to operate without serious failures
availiability is the availibiity of the system to deliver services when requested
security is the ability of a system to protect itself from malicious attacks

mission critical systems: faillure results in failure of some goal directed activited, for example a spacecraft going to mars

business critical systems: system failure results in lots of economic failure, for example amazon selling produts

infrastructure systems: failure results in loss of infastruture 

Critical systems are general a part of a stack of systems, hardware, network
